Discussion on Modern Social Media Trends

Posted by: Kavya | Date: November 17, 2024

Social media has become an integral part of daily life, influencing how we communicate, consume news, and even shape public opinion. Platforms like X (formerly Twitter), TikTok, and Instagram have significantly impacted cultural norms and trends. The question is, where do we draw the line between productive use and over-dependence?

While social media has its benefits, such as connecting people globally and democratizing information, there are challenges like misinformation and mental health concerns. How do we ensure a balanced approach?
